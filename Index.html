About Manickam

We Bring The Soul Of South Indian Cooking To Every Celebration

Founded in 2021 out of a single home kitchen, Manickam has grown into one of Chennai's most requested catering houses — without ever losing the recipes, ratios and rituals that started it all. Every dish is cooked fresh on-site, seasoned to order, and plated with the same care we'd bring to our own family's table.

  • Fresh, hygienic preparation on-site
  • Customisable multi-cuisine menus
  • Indoor, outdoor & destination events
  • Trained service & hospitality staff
